Researcher,
writer,
educator.

A Brazilian-born academic and writer based in Kissimmee, FL. My research asks whose language gets marked as illegitimate — and who bears the cost when automated systems were never designed with them in mind. I work at the intersections of AI literacy, multilingual writing pedagogy, and rhetoric at UCF, while publishing fiction, writing screenplays, and teaching composition. Formerly an Art Director in São Paulo for 8 years.
